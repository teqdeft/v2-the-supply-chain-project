Pay it Forward—Give some Back

We're focused on non-profit capability building because every day is a disaster for people below the poverty line.

Gifts in-kind

Donating gifts and in-kind items is a great way to help others while also getting a tax deduction. When considering a donation, it is important to remember that the IRS has specific rules about what can be deducted. The items must be in good condition, and you must have receipts or other documentation to prove their value. The supply chain project helps you to connect with different nonprofits, can advise what kind of items are applicable for donations, and supports you in your reporting.

MAKE YOUR DONATION

Financial

When you donate to our cause, you help us provide technology, logistic support, training, and consulting services to different non-profits that work with us. By doing so, you make a valuable contribution to our work and help us achieve our mission. We can increase our impact and support more non-profits at local, national, and international levels.

MAKE YOUR CONTRIBUTION

How can you contribute?

Donating to non-profit has many benefits. As a donor, you are entitled to a tax deduction for the total market value of the donated items.

Measuring Success

We measure what matters.

Our metrics capture enterprise value and human well-being—proving impact, improving performance, and driving continuous improvement.

NONPROFITS ONBOARDED

Number of nonprofits connected to the ecosystem.

COMMUNITIES SERVED

Communities receiving resources and support.

MATCHES COMPLETED

Successful matches between supply and need.

VALUE RECOVERED

Total retail value of resources repurposed, shared, or recovered.

COSTS AVOIDED

Cost savings achieved through reuse and efficiency.

TRANSPORTATION CAPACITY MOBILIZED

Miles, trips, and capacity leveraged for mission impact.

WASTE DIVERTED

Tons of materials kept out of landfills and incinerators.

VOLUNTEER & OPERATIONAL CAPACITY CREATED

Volunteer hours, skills, and operational support activated.

INDUSTRY ADOPTION

Businesses and partners actively engaged in the model.

HUMAN WELL-BEING MEASURES

LIVES IMPROVED

People who receive resources and services that improve their quality of life.

SUFFERING REDUCED

Measure reductions in hardship, instability, and unmet essential needs.

WELL-BEING GAINED

Improvements in health, safety, education, and opportunity for individuals and families.

WELLBY™ IMPACT SCORE

A composite well-being measure (WELLBY) that captures multi-dimensional human impact.
