Tier 1: Reshape Demand

We begin with the needs of communities.

Nonprofits function as differently configured retail or distribution operations—focused on serving people, not profit.

Community Needs

Understand real needs on the ground. Include who is affected, what is needed, and why.

Receiving, Storage, & Fulfillment Capacity

Assess ability to receive, store, and fulfill goods. Consider space, equipment, people, and processes. Ensure safe, efficient, and equitable distribution.

Operational Preparedness

Evaluate operational readiness across people, processes, and systems. Plan for surge, continuity, and reliability.

Local Demand Planning

Define assortment and service discipline. Set categories, trade lanes, seasonality, and priorities. Avoid "send us anything" models.

We start by listening—so we can deliver what's needed, not what's left. Right resources. Right place. Right time.
