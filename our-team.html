Our Team

ABOUT US / OUR TEAM

We are building a consortium of donor enterprises.

Our goal is to engage global supply chains to help address the global disparities in access to food, medical equipment, and medical supplies.

By creating a consortium of donor enterprises, service providers, supply chain practitioners, and technology solutions. We will together drive a more intelligent solution for other nonprofits engaged in supporting the delivery of basic human needs.

Our goal—to disrupt and reconfigure the “Human Needs Supply Chain”!

Our aim is to serve those that serve others.

  • Bringing together a group of innovative multinational corporations, regional businesses, and nonprofit partners to develop a technology-based solution to increase supply chain efficiency for Non-Profits dramatically.

Our team is composed of industry experts that aim to change the current reality of our industry for the benefit of humanity.

Our goal is to recruit a wide range of members from across the spectrum of food producers, medical equipment, clothing, and medical supply manufacturers. To include professionals from procurement, demand planning, sales, operations planning, distribution, transportation, and logistics. We will provide a matching service where local needs are paired with available advisory and execution resources.

The Supply Chain Project (TSCP) Multi-Collaborative Marketplace

Did you know that by donating through The Supply Chain Project, you can help support a number of different non-profits? We facilitate contact with different non-profits, so you can be sure that your donation will go to a cause you care about. Plus, you will have access to impact reports that you can customize for your CSR projects. So not only will you be helping out a worthy cause, but you'll also be able to track the impact of your donation.
