Our Technology

ABOUT US / OUR TECHNOLOGY

To answer why it is important to invest in technology?

  • Improved visibility: Technology can help improve visibility into the supply chain, allowing nonprofits to track the movement of goods and materials from supplier to recipient.
  • Enhanced collaboration: Technology can help improve collaboration among stakeholders, allowing them to identify cost-saving opportunities and improve efficiency.
  • Enhanced forecasting: Technology can help organizations improve their forecasting capabilities, allowing them to predict demand and plan accordingly.
  • Improved data accuracy: Technology can help reduce errors and improve the accuracy of supply chain data, leading to better decision-making.
  • Increased sustainability: Technology can help organizations implement sustainable practices in their supply chain, reducing waste and environmental impact.
  • Enhanced security: Technology can help improve supply chain security by providing better tracking and traceability of goods and materials.

Reporting in Non-profits

Benefits of tracking supply chains in the nonprofit sector include:

  • Enhanced transparency about the origins and journey of goods
  • Increased accountability for resources and their allocation
  • Improved data quality for informed decision-making

SCP Tools

  • Kountable connects local distributors with global suppliers, creating transparent and efficient supply chains for nonprofits.
  • Advatix accelerates growth through advanced analytics, logistics optimization, and supply chain management solutions.
  • TSCP Technology Group toolkit for community resilience, helping organizations prepare for and respond to emergencies effectively.
  • Training sessions hosted based on project capacity, providing hands-on learning experiences for nonprofit supply chain teams.
  • Access to customizable reports that help nonprofits measure performance, track KPIs, and demonstrate impact to stakeholders.
