Nonprofits

Optimizing the Nonprofit Supply Chain

Get connected with industry professionals and supply chain providers.

  • At The Supply Chain Project, our goal is to help non-profits maximize their impact by improving their supply chain operations. Whether you need help with logistics, supply chain optimization, data analytics, disaster relief, or procurement, we have the expertise to help you achieve your goals.
GET STARTED

What are our services?

Maximizing impact through streamlined, cost-effective, and socially responsible supply chain solutions through our valued partners.

  • To find out which projects we support, sign up and check your eligibility.
  • When you join, our validation experts will identify which programs your organization can participate in.
  • Fill out the form, and you will be added to our database. You will get newsletters with relevant information and support in your projects.
BECOME A MEMBER

What kind of services are available?

For Nonprofits, you can request logistical support (transport, storage, and related), training, and advisory in optimization projects.

For Companies, you can request advisory for donations entry and tax declarations. You can request support for your CSR projects and employee engagement in volunteer projects.

For Professionals and students, you get access to training, news, events, and opportunities for consultancy and networking.
